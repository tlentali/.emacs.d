# org contact

## To add a contact :

```text
M-x org-capure
c
fill the form
```

The birthday (`yyyy-mm-dd`) of the contact is used in org-agenda.

## To look for contact

```text
M-x org-contact
type the name (or partial name) you are looking for
```
